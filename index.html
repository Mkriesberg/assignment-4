MBTA Communities Map
In 2019, Massachusetts passed a law - the MBTA Communities Act - that requires cities and towns served by the MBTA to create a new zoning district near an MBTA stop that allows multi-family housing. The first set of rezonings must happen in so-called rapid transit communities.
The rapid transit communities are Braintree, Brookline, Cambridge, Chelsea, Everett, Malden, Medford, Milton, Newton, Quincy, Revere, and Somerville.
Click on a rapid transit community to see how many new housing units it must zone for under the new law.
